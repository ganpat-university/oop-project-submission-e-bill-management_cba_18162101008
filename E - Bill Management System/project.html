About Power

Sunpower Power Endless Limited is a holding company. The company is engaged in providing electric power generation by coal-based thermal power plants and coal trading. Its business activity is undertaken at Ahmedabad Thermal Power Plant of the company in Gujarat and Thermal Power Plant of its subsidiaries at Daishar (Maharashtra), and at Kawai (Rajasthan).

Vision

To be a world class leader in businesses that enrich lives and contribute to nations in building infrastructure through sustainable value creation.
